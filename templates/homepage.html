Quinn Corporate Chambers

uinn Corporate Chambers is a newly established corporate firm in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania. Since its establishment, the firm has evolved from a general practice law firm to a specialized law firm with a wide range of interdisciplinary expertise, which gives it superiority in resolving clients’ problems in a timely and efficient manner. However the firm is also offering Advocacy services as well as representation in all matters involving the law, taxation, international trade, investments and related fields, banking, insurance, corporate and all other related legal matters.

We have represented and still represent a spectrum of clientele that includes businesses, small and medium sized companies, financial firms, investors and high net worth private clients. We have engaged in a broad-based business transactional planning and structuring practice. Our team combines sophisticated planning concepts with practical approaches to achieve our clients’ goals. We advise businesses at all stages of their development, from the initial choice of entity structure and issues with daily operations, through acquisitive and growth transactions, to dispositions and succession planning. We offer tax advice on all types of business transactional issues, including the choice of entity structure, restructuring and terminations.

The Firm has been committed to the complementary goals of being both aggressive and cost-effective in pursuit of our clients’ interests. Because every client has individualized goals and resources, we devise and execute individualized strategies to protect the client’s rights and promote the client’s objectives.

FIELD OF EXPERTISE:

Corporate and Commercial Law are at the heart of the firm. We advise on all matters of Corporate Law in Tanzania. We cater for all legal services from incorporation of the corporate entity to the dissolution of such entity. We also provide corporate secretarial services and corporate statutory compliance services, to ensure that our client do comply with the legal regime and good governance to the corporate boards. We provide expert legal advice and practical assistance to companies and their boards of directors on corporate practice and other compliance matters.

We provide our clients with well-informed legal advice on matters of finance ranging from banking matters, insurance and capital markets. We advise clients on legal matters relating to their dealings with banking and financial institutions, insurance companies and representations in the Dar es Salaam Stock of Exchange. Our services range from first and second loan facilities; acquisition financings; senior and subordinated bridge financings; recapitalization financings; asset-backed financings; mezzanine financings; workouts, restructurings and exit financings; private placements; and investment grade financings.

The firm advises clients when buying and selling commercial, industrial or residential property or in matters involving owners’ corporations. We are also experienced in matters involving retail or commercial leasing. When dealing with property matters, our expertise in conveyancing, finance and refinancing, subdivisions, leasing and planning laws will serve to protect all client’s interests. You can also be assured that when property is affected by family or estate disputes, we have the relevant skills and expertise in resolving such matters. Our key areas of expertise and experience includes Residential Conveyancing, Commercial and industrial Conveyancing, Building contracts, Creation and removal of caveats, Creation and removal of covenants, Creation and removal of easements, Financing and refinancing, Retail and commercial leasing, Planning applications and disputes, Subdivisions and developments.

Our firm regularly advise business enterprises, boards and company management teams on employment contracts and staff policy handbooks, labour relations and trade unions, employee transfer, and issues of recruitment, retention and termination. Our lawyers also have extensive experience in providing commercially focused advice on employment incentives. We have assisted local and international clients with implementation and structuring of pension schemes, share plans, and compensation claims and benefits. Our advisory services cover all aspects of Employment contracts, Recruitment and retention, Employee transfers, Retrenchment, dismissal and redundancy, Employment litigation and alternative dispute resolution, Health and safety, Immigration and work permits, Non-compete provision agreements, Employee incentive and share ownership plans.

We provide businesses and individuals with sophisticated and comprehensive tax advice in the areas of corporate tax, individual tax, withholding tax, VAT, and all other indirect taxes. We advise clients on a wide range of tax issues including business transactional planning and structuring, real estate, finance, compensation planning, mergers, acquisitions, divestitures and restructurings, tax controversy, and international taxation.

The Firm has a clear understanding of the investment climate in Tanzania as well as an extensive commercial law practice related to local and international business transactions. We also cover all aspects of investment in the country ranging from drafting contracts and agreements to infrastructure, taxation, and stock exchange. We offer all services including Share/Stock purchase agreements and shareholders agreements; Indemnity and a variety of investment agreements; and Applications for investment incentives.

Entertainment law is the sum total of several different types of law that are relevant to the entertainment industry including transactional law and litigation. That is why we make sure that our clients are well informed of their rights as well as obligations needed to be fulfilled under entertainment law. In this area we deal with issues like Drafting and negotiating development and production contracts, such as writer agreements, talent agreements, and recording agreements, Facilitating and negotiating distribution deals for an entertainment project, Working on financing agreements for sponsorships, bank loans, co-production investments, grants, and other types of investments, Preparing form agreements for clients, such as appearance releases, location releases, and license agreements and Reviewing contracts and other legal documents to analyze rights issues or other business or legal questions.

Over and above the above-mentioned areas of expertise the firm also boasts its expertise in the following streams, Litigation, Competition Law, Consumer Law, Intellectual Property Law, Family Law, Capital Market Law, Wildlife Law and Human Rights Law.
