Quinn Corporate Chambers

Quinn Corporate Chambers is a newly established corporate firm in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania. Since its establishment, the firm has evolved from a general practice law firm to a specialized law firm with a wide range of interdisciplinary expertise, which gives it superiority in resolving clients’ problems in a timely and efficient manner. However the firm is also offering Advocacy services as well as representation in all matters involving the law, taxation, international trade, investments and related fields, banking, insurance, corporate and all other related legal matters.

We have represented and still represent a spectrum of clientele that includes businesses, small and medium sized companies, financial firms, investors and high net worth private clients. We have engaged in a broad-based business transactional planning and structuring practice. Our team combines sophisticated planning concepts with practical approaches to achieve our clients’ goals. We advise businesses at all stages of their development, from the initial choice of entity structure and issues with daily operations, through acquisitive and growth transactions, to dispositions and succession planning. We offer tax advice on all types of business transactional issues, including the choice of entity structure, restructuring and terminations.

The Firm has been committed to the complementary goals of being both aggressive and cost-effective in pursuit of our clients’ interests. Because every client has individualized goals and resources, we devise and execute individualized strategies to protect the client’s rights and promote the client’s objectives.

Legal Status

This is one of the fast-growing corporate firms in the Dar Es Salaam. It has its offices on Plot Number 43, Cameroon Street, within Dar Es Salaam City. The firm was initially registered with the Registrar of Companies on 2nd day of January, 2020 vide Certificate of Registration No 465085.

FIELD OF EXPERTISE:

We advise on all matters of Corporate Law in Tanzania. We cater for all legal services from incorporation of the corporate entity to the dissolution of such entity. We also provide corporate secretarial services and corporate statutory compliance services, to ensure that our client do comply with the legal regime and good governance to the corporate boards. We provide expert legal advice and practical assistance to companies and their boards of directors on corporate practice and other compliance matters.

Our services range from first and second loan facilities; acquisition financings; senior and subordinated bridge financings; recapitalization financings; asset-backed financings; mezzanine financings; workouts, restructurings and exit financings; private placements; and investment grade financings. We also work with lawyers in other corporate firms to provide unified representation and integrated legal services. We also deal with recovery of loans, this is backed up with experienced litigation lawyers

The firm believes in efficiency of the Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) system. Whenever appropriate, the firm employs different forms of ADR to bring about quick resolution of disputes to the relief of the firm clientele.
