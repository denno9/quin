CORPORATE & COMMERCIAL LAW

We advise on all matters of Corporate Law in Tanzania. We cater for all legal services from incorporation of the corporate entity to the dissolution of such entity. We provide our clients with well-informed legal advice on matters of finance ranging from banking matters, insurance and capital markets. Entertainment law is the sum total of several different types of law that are relevant to the entertainment industry including transactional law and litigation.That is why we make sure that our clients are well informed of their rights as well as obligations needed to be fulfilled under entertainment law.
